Hi, I'm Matt.

A bit about who, and why I am.

Figuring out my first stop-motion animation (at around the age of 8), witnessed by scrolling through the preview screen of a digital camera was a formative moment. I briefly had the thought that I had invented animation. This turned out not to be the case, but despite this, an important realisation was made. Making things with technology is my idea of a good time. Since then, I have accumlated a wide range of skills and experiences in a variety of creative software.


Some highlights include:

  • Providing teaching support in video-production summer programme at the Digital Media Academy a programme aimed providing students technical and creative skills using industry-standard software and hardware.
  • My accumulated video and illustration content posted on instagram and YouTube (short films, animations, illustrations etc.).
  • Modelling and rigging a playable character using 3DS Max, Maya, Z Brush, in Unreal Engine.

Fast forward to today, and I am enrolled in edX's Front-end Web-development course on track to receive my W3Cx's Front-End Web Developer Professional Certificate, and am hoping to take the skills I have learned to the next level in a challenging new professional environment!
